WHO WE ARE


PEDS MOTO exists as a digital museum dedicated to the helmets worn by the most iconic figures in motorcycle racing. These helmets are not treated as accessories, but as historical artifacts shaped by speed, danger, and identity.

Every design reflects a moment in time — a championship season, a fierce rivalry, a crash survived, or a victory earned by fractions of a second. Scratches, color schemes, and symbols tell stories that statistics alone never could.

Our purpose is preservation. In an era where racing history moves fast and fades quickly, we slow things down. We present helmets with respect, clarity, and space — allowing fans to study, appreciate, and understand the mindset of the riders who wore them at the limit.

This platform is built for enthusiasts who value legacy, detail, and authenticity. No noise. No clutter. Just racing history, presented the way it deserves to be seen.
